SlopScale

SlopScale generates scale, arpeggio, and progression practice material as Slopsmith-compatible chart data. It is designed to reuse existing Slopsmith renderers instead of maintaining a separate game renderer.

Renderer requirement

The current preview adapter prefers the bundled 3D Highway renderer exposed as window.slopsmithViz_highway_3d. 2D/tab output is routed through compatible renderer factories when Slopsmith exposes one.

Data files

Saved presets are stored under CONFIG_DIR/plugin_data/slopscale/presets.json.
